Apex Drifters: Project Game Description

By Apex Drifters Group 11/04/2023

Welcome to Apex Drifters, a thrilling race against time and obstacles, developed by innovative minds at HES-SO VS. In this engaging web-based game, players navigate a sleek car down an endless road, dodging obstacles and collecting fuel to keep the journey alive.

This game introduces a unique twist to the classic racing genre - an ever-depleting fuel gauge that demands strategic maneuvering and quick reflexes to ensure the car doesn't run out of gas. With every obstacle hit, the stakes get higher as precious fuel is lost. Can you keep the tank full and the wheels turning?

The source code of the application is available on the GitHub of the project. Join us in our development journey and let's push the boundaries of web gaming together. game mechanics: - add different kind of cars/vehicles with different stats - add coins to unlock new vehicles and/or power-ups (power-ups can only be used for one race) - drag and drop -> add power-ups to your vehicle in the home page - during the game, power-ups can be activated by pushing a button, so you can buy a few power-ups and use them when you need them and also possibly stack them (every power-up is activated by a different button) - power ups: shields, speed boost, invincible for 5 seconds, emergency fuel
